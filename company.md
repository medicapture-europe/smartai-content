## 1. üè¢ Company Overview

MediCapture, founded in **2002**, provides advanced medical imaging solutions.

* **Motto:** "Medical Imaging Made Easy."
* **Mission:** To provide user-friendly, efficient, high-quality products at affordable prices for healthcare professionals.
* **Product Scope:** Designs and manufactures medical imaging systems, video recorders, video signal converters, and monitors.
* **Applications:** Used in laparoscopic surgeries, endoscopies, microscopy, gastroenterology, ophthalmology, retinal surgeries, and more.
* **Target Environments:** From small medical office environments to state-of-the-art operating rooms.
* **Versatility:** Products can also be used in **veterinary applications**.
* **Global Reach:**
    * **Headquarters:** USA.
    * **Offices:** Europe and Taiwan.
    * Maintains an international network of partners and distributors for accessibility, support, and training.

### RMA Process Summary
1.  **Request Form:** Contact MediCapture US or Europe for the RMA form.
2.  **Fill Form:** Complete the form with requested details (Model, S/N, Problem, etc.).
3.  **Shipment:** Return the unit to the specified address on the RMA form (Florida for Americas; Sittard for Europe/Others), enclosing a copy of the RMA form.
4.  **Evaluation:** MediCapture evaluates the unit and provides a repair estimate.
5.  **Repair/Return:**
    * **Approved:** Unit is repaired (parts + labor). Return shipping is customer-paid, except for US domestic shipping on approved US repairs.
    * **Unapproved/No Issue Found:** Customer pays return shipping and any applicable inspection fee.
6.  **Payment:** Pay per account terms or by credit card upon repair approval.

### Important RMA Notes
* Only send MediCapture devices unless instructed otherwise.
* **Do not send patient records or recordings.** They will be destroyed immediately.
* Customer covers shipping costs **to** MediCapture.
* Repaired units have a **6-month warranty**.
