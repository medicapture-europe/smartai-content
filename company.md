# **MediCapture Company Overview**

## **Company Overview**

MediCapture was founded in 2002 and provides medical imaging solutions for healthcare professionals. The company designs and manufactures advanced medical imaging systems, medical video recorders, medical video signal converters, and medical monitors for use in:

* Laparoscopic surgeries  
* Endoscopies  
* Microscopy  
* Gastroenterology procedures  
* Ophthalmology  
* Retinal surgeries  
* Veterinary applications, and more.

### **Motto & Commitment**

**"Medical Imaging Made Easy"**

MediCapture is committed to making medical imaging easy by providing user-friendly, efficient, high-quality products at affordable prices for healthcare professionals. With a product range designed to meet the needs of small medical office environments as well as state-of-the-art operating rooms, MediCapture delivers versatile imaging solutions that offer a seamless workflow and are easy to use.

### **Global Reach**

The company maintains a global reach through offices worldwide, including:

* **Headquarters:** United States (USA)  
* **Regional Offices:** Europe and Taiwan  
* **Global Network:** International partners and distributors ensuring accessibility, support, and training across various regions.

## **RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) & Repair Instructions**

1. **Request RMA Form:** Contact US MediCapture (888-922-7887) or Europe (+31 46 2081124, service.europe@medicapture.com) to request the form.  
2. **Print the Form.**  
3. **Fill Out the Required Details (takes \~10–15 minutes):**  
   * **RMA \#** (Prefilled by MediCapture)  
   * **Customer \#** (Prefilled by MediCapture)  
   * Date Requested  
   * Company Name & Contact Person  
   * Company Address, Telephone, Fax, and Email Address  
   * Ship via and Shipping Reference  
   * Model, Serial Number, and Quantity  
   * **Problem Found:** Describe the problem and replication steps in detail.  
4. **Prepare Package:** Make a copy of the completed RMA form for your records and enclose another copy inside the box with the return.  
5. **Ship to the Appropriate Address:**  
   * **North & South America:** Return the RMA unit to the Florida address specified on the RMA form.  
   * **Europe, Middle East, India, Africa, Russia & CIS:** Return the goods in their original packaging to the address in the top-left corner of the RMA form.  
6. **Evaluation:** Upon receipt, MediCapture will evaluate the unit and contact you with a repair estimate.  
7. **Approval:** If you approve the repair costs (covering parts and labor), the unit will be repaired and shipped back.  
8. **Shipping Costs:**  
   * Return shipping costs are paid by the customer, except for US domestic shipping for US customers who approve repair costs.  
   * If repair costs are rejected or no issues are found, the customer pays return shipping.  
   * *Note for Europe, Middle East, India, Africa, Russia, and CIS:* A **EUR 250.00 inspection fee** applies if repair costs are not approved or no issue is found.

### **Important Notes on RMA:**

* Payment is due per account terms or by credit card upon repair approval.  
* **Do not send accessories** or non-MediCapture equipment unless explicitly instructed.  
* **IMPORTANT WARNING:** Do not send patient records or recordings with the unit. They will be **destroyed immediately without question and cannot be recovered**.  
* Customer covers inbound shipping costs to MediCapture.

## **Disinfection & Cleaning Instructions**

All MediCapture products are designed and manufactured by an in-house ISO 13485 facility. To maintain hygienic standards, follow the guidelines below:

### **Preparation:**

* Before disinfection, the device must be cleaned to remove any debris. Debris can be removed using a dry, clean cloth.

### **Disinfectant Guidelines:**

* Use disinfectants approved by the **U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)** or **U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)**.  
* **Manufacturer Recommended Disinfectant:** Ethyl Alcohol, 70% (e.g., Becton Dickinson BD Alcohol Swabs).

### **Disinfection Steps:**

1. Pour approximately **5ml of ethyl alcohol (70%)** on a sterile cotton swab or use a pre-packaged 70% alcohol prep swab.  
2. Swipe the surface area of the device.  
3. Do not allow disinfectant to come into contact with any electrical connections on the back faceplate, or the USB ports on the front faceplate.  
4. **IMPORTANT\!** Make sure that absolutely no humidity enters the device. Do not use any cleaning agents, scouring agents, or harsh solvents on this device.
